Welcome to !

Created in the 5th of january, 2024 by Pyrosfere, this website's purpose is to index redesigns of existing flags of existing countries, provinces, departments, cantons, counties, municipalities, cities, towns, settlements, villages, etc., geographical locations like hills, rivers, bodies of water in general, mountains, peaks, etc., geographical, cultural or/and "political" regions, organizations, concepts, planets, peoples, etc., and of fictional ones, at the same time indexing official, existing and/or historical flags.

Contributing

You can contribute to our website in various ways, and no one is useless here! Here is a list of what you can help in:

  • making pages
  • adding your redesign to a page
  • searching for official flags in state websites
  • finding bugs
  • giving feedback
  • sharing on social media
  • researching the official meaning of flags
  • adding a popular unnoficial meaning to flags
  • suggesting pages
  • adding flags from other sources
  • and many more!

Making pages

To make pages in , you must follow the guide of how to make pages to learn how to structure the pages, what pages are allowed and what aren't. After you wrote your article in HTML, you now need to fork our repository, make a new page in its designated place in the file structure, and then make a pull request. I will probably accept it in some time.
